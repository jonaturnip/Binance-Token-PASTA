WHAT IS OUR MISSION?

Circa December 29, 2021, a copy$PASTA coin is stealth launched on the Binance Smart Chain by a mysterious developer. The contract includes the name of a Telegram channel (PastaToken). However, there is no sign to be found of this enigmatic Telegram. The developer simply bestowed us with a simple command: “Power is in the People.”

A few /biz/ threads surface, but shilling is minimal. This was because the developer had locked the liquidity of Pasta for a mere nine days. At this point in time it seemed Pasta was just another pump and dump with a funny - albeit long - name. Even so there was a fragment of hope shimmering beyond the horizon: the contract included an assurance that there was a possibility that the liquidity lock would be extended if certain conditions were met.

It was then that a group of dedicated soldiers set out to get the mission cleared and extend the liquidity lock. The oracular developer promised he would extend the liquidity lock on January 1st, of the New Year. So when that day came it was with great surprise to everyone, bar a few, that the developer had honoured his covenant to the people by extending the lock for 69 years.

Since then Pasta has been blessed with a talented and dedicated community willing to always take things to the next level. A community ready to take the world by storm with original ideas and concepts. Pasta is a force to be reckoned with; it is a coin for the people.
